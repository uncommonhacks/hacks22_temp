Uncommon Hacks!

What are We All About?

Founded in 2016, Uncommon Hacks is a weekend-long celebration of technology where college students from everywhere come to Chicago to make fun, random, and innovative tech-related projects with friends old and new. Every year, we work hard to create a hackathon where all are welcome, regardless of experience or background, and where outside-the-box projects and creative thinking are encouraged. For our fifth annual hackathon last winter, we hosted more than 200 of the brightest and most highly motivated students from Universities around the United States. You can get an idea of what goes on at Uncommon Hacks by checking out our recap video from 2019: https://youtu.be/p2X63fVdIIY.

Location, Place, Survey

Hackathons are invention marathons where attendees collaborate on a project over the course of a weekend! hackers will be in a fun, supportive environment with the necessary tools provided to create excellent projects. Newbies are encouraged, normies aren't. This is a safe space to create the weirdest, quirkiest, and most amazing projects ever. University and high school students from anywhere are welcome, even if you're not a computer science major or have never been to a hackathon before!
